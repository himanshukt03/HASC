## 💡 Pro Tips for Maximum Backlink Quality

1. **Focus on relevance over quantity**
   - Healthcare > General blogs
   - Senior care > Tech blogs
   - Psychiatric/behavioral health > Unrelated topics

2. **Prioritize high DA sites**
   - Target DA 50+, ideally 60+
   - 10 DA-60 links > 100 DA-20 links

3. **Get founder/team mentions**
   - Person-focused backlinks (bio links) = higher trust
   - Expert credentials matter

4. **Build relationships, not just links**
   - Follow up with contacts
   - Offer value first (guest posts, interviews)
   - Long-term partnerships > one-off links

5. **Diversify anchor text**
   - "Health Alliance SoCal"
   - "psychiatric services los angeles"
   - "assisted living psychiatry"
   - "HASC"
   - Branded + keyword mix
