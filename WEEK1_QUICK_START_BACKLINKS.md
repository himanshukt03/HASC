## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Use same business email for all directories** - Easier to track & manage
2. **Keep descriptions consistent** - Same tone/message across all sites
3. **Use keywords naturally** - "assisted living psychiatry", "mental health care", etc.
4. **Upload high-quality photos** - Professionalism matters for authority
5. **Respond quickly to directory emails** - Approval often depends on speed
6. **Save confirmation emails** - Proof of submission for tracking
7. **Set calendar reminders** - Check status in 2-3 weeks for each directory

---

## ❌ What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't use keyword stuffing (looks like spam)
- ❌ Don't submit identical descriptions (personalize for each directory)
- ❌ Don't forget to add website URL (this is where the backlink comes from)
- ❌ Don't ignore verification emails (complete verification immediately)
- ❌ Don't submit then ignore (follow up if not approved in 2-3 weeks)
- ❌ Don't use outdated phone/email (use current business contact)
